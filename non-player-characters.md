## Robin Kerensky

- Robin is an assistant instructor at the Lightweavers Guild teaching close quarters combat to beginner arcane apprentices (AA1).
- Robin has a wife who supports his endeavors, and they share a quiet life within the Nether City.
- He is friends with another assistant instructor named Trayzen.

## Alice Paulsen

- Alice handles the administrative affairs at the Veyron guild. She sometimes fills in at the reception desk.
- Alice is in her early thirties, with an air of confidence that comes from years of experience in managing affairs.
